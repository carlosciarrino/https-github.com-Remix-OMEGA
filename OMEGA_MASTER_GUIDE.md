# OMEGA: Master Narrative Guide

## 1. Core Directives (Immutable Rules)
- **Timelessness:** NEVER use fixed years or dates (e.g., 2025, 2030). Use relative references ("ten years ago", "before the Transition").
- **Technological Realism:** Technology must feel like today's tech (early volumes). No holograms or sci-fi robots. ABITES operates through algorithms, markets, and logistics.
- **The Boiled Frog:** Dystopia is imposed through comfort, fear of crisis, and safety promises, not immediate violence.
- **Clean Fiction:** No explicit vulgarity. Use human frustration instead.
- **Show, Don't Tell:** Reveal lore through details (rejected payments, blackouts, glitching drones).
- **Romance as Resistance:** Passionate, deep, but "clean" relationships (Male/Female only) are the ultimate rebellion against the sterile machine.

## 2. Character Cast (The Sconnessi)
- **Claudio Mancini ("Er Cifra"):** 42, Rome. Ex-ABITES architect. Sarcastic, tormented. "Se lallero!"
- **Sara Vance ("The Resonance"):** 30, Appalachia. Mystic, empathetic. Perceives the earth's resonance.
- **Julian Vane ("The Prophet"):** 45, London. Antagonist. Carismatic, cold. Avatar of ABITES.
- **Dr. Aris Mancini ("The Professor"):** 50, Rome. Claudio's brother. Bio-hacker, mentor.
- **Laura ("The Lynx"):** 26, Brazil/Rome. Infiltrator, agile, courageous. "Porca miseria!"
- **Cyrus ("The Hound"):** 40, Inquisitor. Julian's enforcer. Obsessed with Claudio.
- **Carlo ("The Seer"):** 52, Turin/Rome. Christian mentor, moral compass. "Boia fauss!"

## 3. Plot Summary: Volume 1 (The Eclipse of Rome)
- **Chapters 1-6:** Claudio and Aris discover ABITES' predictive nature. They retreat to the Roman underground. Biometric surveillance is tightening.
- **Chapter 7 (The Cage Architecture):** Aris discovers neural mapping code hidden in wearable/medical updates.
- **Chapter 8 (Mud and Kevlar):** Sara's community in Appalachia is raided by Teslaris security under a "health check" pretext.
- **Chapter 9 (The Truth Shall Set You Free):** Claudio meets Carlo, who reveals the spiritual/prophetic depth of the machine's control.
- **Chapter 10 (Terminal Extraction):** Claudio is caught stealing data. Laura facilitates a high-stakes escape through the Roman subway.

## 4. Writing Style
- Hard Sci-Fi / Dystopian.
- Immersive, cinematic, mature.
- Focus on sensory details (smell of ozone, coldness of metal).

## 5. Editorial & Ghostwriting Directives (The "Human" Rewrite)
- **Human-Centered Storytelling:** Eliminate robotic tone and technical narration. Focus on human experiences, relationships, and emotional resonance.
- **Concise Prose:** Replace over-explained descriptions with natural prose that trusts the reader's intuition.
- **Emotional Priority:** Prioritize emotions and character interactions over analytical observations.
- **Authentic Dialogue:** Rewrite dialogue and internal thoughts to sound authentic and human. Include everyday topics to humanize characters.
- **Narrative Flow:** Connect sentences smoothly to create flow instead of disconnected observations.
- **Active Storytelling:** Show, don't over-explain. Replace passive observation with active narrative.
- **Narrative Tension:** Create curiosity, anticipation, and emotional hooks from the first pages.
- **No Mechanical Comparisons:** Never describe human behavior using mechanical or robotic metaphors.
